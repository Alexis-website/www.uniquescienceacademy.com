About Us

Welcome to Unique Science Academy, the school with a glorious history and tradition spanning over fifteen years! Our dedicated focus on students, commitment to quality and dedication to continuous improvement strengthen our students to excel in all areas of life.

Unique Science Academy has successfully negotiated unchartered territories with the prime aim of restructuring the future of our society and country.

Our greatest strength lies in our commitment of singular vision to bring out the very best in every child.

Unique Science Academy is located in Basti, Uttar Pradesh administered by “Unique Social & Educational Society”. It is a co-educational and day school which opened its door with a humble beginning in July 2004.

The institution has since then grown and is progressing by leaps and bounds with more than 2,500 students on the roll, catering to the educational needs of Basti city and neighboring areas of Basti district in the state of Uttar Pradesh.
